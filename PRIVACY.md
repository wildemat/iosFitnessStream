# Privacy Policy

**Fitness Stream**
Last updated: February 26, 2026

## Overview

Fitness Stream does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device.

## Data Handling

- **Workout and health data** (heart rate, calories, distance, GPS, etc.) is read from Apple HealthKit and sent only to the HTTP endpoint you configure in the app. No data is sent to us or any third party.
- **API key** is stored locally in the iOS Keychain on your device. It is never transmitted except as a header to your configured endpoint.
- **Endpoint URL** is stored locally on your device in app preferences.

## No Analytics or Tracking

Fitness Stream contains no analytics SDKs, no crash reporting services, no advertising frameworks, and no third-party code that collects user data.

## No Accounts

The app does not require or support user accounts. There is no sign-up, login, or cloud sync.

## Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party services. All network requests go exclusively to the endpoint URL you provide.
